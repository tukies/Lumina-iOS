# Privacy Policy for Lumina

Last updated: 20 March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Lumina ("the app") handles information when you use it.

Lumina is a smart-lighting control app that analyses audio in real time and communicates with compatible lighting devices on your local network. We have written this policy in plain English so you can understand what the app does with your information.

## 1. Summary

Lumina is designed to keep your data on your device as far as possible.

In normal use, Lumina:

- accesses your microphone only to analyse audio in real time so it can drive lighting effects;
- communicates with compatible lighting devices and bridges on your local network;
- stores your room configuration, scenes, gradients, lighting settings and certain device details locally on your device;
- stores Philips Hue bridge credentials in the Apple Keychain on your device;
- does not include third-party advertising SDKs;
- does not send analytics, tracking data or marketing profiles to external analytics services.

## 2. Information Lumina May Access or Store

### Microphone / audio input

Lumina uses microphone or audio input access to analyse sound in real time. This is used to detect information such as beat, BPM, pitch, energy and frequency-band activity so that connected lights can react to audio.

Audio analysis is intended to happen locally on your device. Lumina does not need to upload raw microphone audio to provide its core lighting features.

### Local network and device information

Lumina connects to compatible devices on your local network, including products such as WLED, Philips Hue, Nanoleaf and LIFX.

To make those connections work, the app may access or store:

- device names;
- IP addresses;
- fixture layouts and panel layouts;
- pixel counts, stream settings and related device configuration;
- Hue entertainment area identifiers;
- Nanoleaf authorisation tokens;
- LIFX MAC addresses.

This information is used only to discover, pair, configure, identify and control your lighting devices.

### Credentials and authentication data

If you pair Lumina with a Philips Hue bridge, the app stores the Hue username and client key in the Apple Keychain on your device.

If you pair Lumina with a Nanoleaf device, the app stores the Nanoleaf authentication token locally on your device as part of the saved device configuration.

These credentials are used so the app can reconnect to your devices and send lighting commands without asking you to pair again every time.

### App settings and saved content

Lumina stores certain information locally on your device so your setup is preserved between sessions. This may include:

- room configuration;
- fixtures and device mapping;
- saved scenes and scene sequences;
- custom gradients;
- selected lighting mode and related settings;
- audio calibration values and preferences;
- background-mode preference;
- selected audio input device.

### Optional diagnostic logging

The codebase includes optional debug-style audio logging that can write audio-analysis values to a local CSV file when explicitly enabled in the app's runtime environment. This is intended for testing or development use and is not required for ordinary app use.

## 3. How We Use Information

Lumina uses the information above to:

- provide audio-reactive lighting features;
- connect to and control compatible lighting hardware;
- save your layout, scenes and preferences;
- restore your setup when you reopen the app;
- support pairing, identification and streaming features for supported devices;
- maintain app performance and reliability.

## 4. What Lumina Does Not Intend To Do

Based on the current app implementation, Lumina does not appear to:

- sell your personal information;
- build advertising profiles about you;
- include third-party advertising networks;
- send behaviour-based analytics to external analytics platforms as part of its core functionality.

## 5. Sharing of Information

Lumina primarily uses information locally on your device and on your local network.

The app shares information only where needed to operate with your lighting hardware, for example by sending commands or authentication requests to devices or bridges that you have chosen to connect on your local network.

We do not state that no information is ever shared in every possible circumstance, because information may also be disclosed if required by law, regulation, legal process or the protection of rights, safety and security.

## 6. Legal Bases for Processing

If and to the extent U.K. data protection law applies, the legal bases relied on for processing are:

- performance of a contract: to provide the app's core features that you request;
- legitimate interests: to operate, secure and improve the app and connected-device functionality;
- consent: where permission is required, such as microphone access under your device settings.

You can withdraw device permissions, such as microphone access, at any time in your device settings, although some features may no longer work.

## 7. Data Retention

Lumina keeps locally stored information for as long as it remains on your device, or until you remove it by:

- deleting saved scenes or configuration within the app;
- unpairing or removing devices;
- clearing app data, where your platform allows that;
- uninstalling the app.

Items stored in the Apple Keychain, such as Philips Hue credentials, may persist until removed by the app, by your system, or when associated app/keychain data is deleted under Apple's platform behaviour.

## 8. Security

Lumina uses platform-provided mechanisms where available, including the Apple Keychain for Philips Hue credentials.

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure. You are responsible for securing access to your device and your local network, including smart-home devices connected to that network.

## 9. International Transfers

Lumina's core features are intended to operate locally on your device and local network. If you contact the developer or use third-party services outside the app, separate data handling and international transfer arrangements may apply to those communications or services.

## 10. Children's Privacy

Lumina is not directed specifically at children. We do not knowingly seek to collect personal information from children through the app's core functionality.

## 11. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, including under U.K. GDPR or similar laws, you may have rights relating to personal data, such as the right to request access, correction, deletion, restriction or objection.

Because Lumina is designed to store most information locally on your device, many privacy choices can be exercised directly by managing permissions, saved content and app installation on your device.

## 12. Third-Party Devices and Services

Lumina works with third-party hardware and ecosystems, including WLED, Philips Hue, Nanoleaf and LIFX. Your use of those devices and services is also subject to their own privacy policies and terms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party products or services.

## 13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we do, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after an updated policy becomes effective means the updated policy will apply, to the extent permitted by law.

## 14. Contact
